## Prerequisites

- The Request should be sent

## Directions

1. Once you're done with testing your first request, navigate to the `Save` button in besides the `Send` button.
2. Click the `Save` button.
3. Enter the `title` and `description` of the request.
4. Choose a `project` or a `module` you wish to save your request to.
5. Hit Save.

There you go! You can now manage and modify your saved requests later from left Project panel.
