## Prerequisites

- A configured and connected Socket.io
- Active Event listener

> *Preparing emitters collection will help you in the future for quick testing of different bodies and messages within a single click.*


## Directions

1. After adding the Emitters name and Argument, instead of click on Emit, click on `Emit and Save`.
2. You can notice, in the `Emitter collection` list all the Emitter that you sent started appending into the list.

3. Whenever you want to test the In future, just open the message by click on `<` infront of `emitter name` 
4. Click on the `emit` button.


Awesome! Your meesage collection ready with diffrent message payload.
